# **App Name**: DankDrops

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Filtering: Browse a curated selection of apparel, accessories, and other merchandise. Filter products by categories like new arrivals, clothing type, or brand.
- Detailed Product Pages: View high-resolution product images, comprehensive descriptions, pricing, and available sizes/colors for each item.
- Shopping Cart Management: Add desired products to a shopping cart, adjust quantities, and easily remove items before checkout.
- User Authentication & Profile: Users can create accounts, log in securely, manage their personal information, and view their order history.
- Intuitive Product Search: Search for specific products using keywords or product names across the entire catalog.
- AI-Powered Product Recommendations: An AI tool will suggest complementary products to users based on their current browsing history or items in their shopping cart, enhancing discovery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Palette based on streetwear culture and bold branding with a dark scheme. Primary color: Deep Violet (#7E2ADB) to evoke a premium and cool street vibe. Background color: Very Dark Violet (#211928) for a sleek, modern base. Accent color: Bright Blue (#6088EE) to provide energetic contrast and highlight calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-influenced street aesthetic. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a neutral, objective feel for product descriptions and general text.
- Utilize clean, minimalist, and geometric icons that align with a modern streetwear brand's identity for navigation, cart, and user profile.
- Adopt a grid-based layout for product listings, prioritizing large, impactful product imagery. Product pages should be clean and allow easy access to details and add-to-cart functionality.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on product cards and buttons, along with smooth transitions for page changes and cart interactions, enhancing user experience without being distracting.
